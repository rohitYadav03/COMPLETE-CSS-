justify-items:
Purpose: Aligns grid items horizontally (along the inline axis).
How it works: It adjusts the position of the items within their grid cells along the horizontal axis (left to right by default).
Values:
start: Aligns items to the left of the grid cell.
end: Aligns items to the right of the grid cell.
center: Aligns items to the center of the grid cell.
stretch (default): Stretches items to fill the entire width of the grid cell


align-items:
Purpose: Aligns grid items vertically (along the block axis).
How it works: It adjusts the position of the items within their grid cells along the vertical axis (top to bottom by default).
Values:
start: Aligns items to the top of the grid cell.
end: Aligns items to the bottom of the grid cell.
center: Aligns items to the center of the grid cell.
stretch (default): Stretches items to fill the entire height of the grid cell.
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
flexbox:
justify-content: Aligns the flex items within the container (x-axis).
align-items: Aligns the flex items within the container (y-axis).

CSS Grid:
justify-items: Aligns the grid items inside their grid cells (x-axis).
align-items: Aligns the grid items inside their grid cells (y-axis).
